About Me

Hello! I'm Aman, and my fascination with flight began at a young age. This passion led me to pursue a B.Tech in Aerospace Engineering at one of India's premier institutions, IIT Kanpur. My coursework has provided me with a strong foundation in fluid dynamics, structural mechanics, and control systems.

Beyond the classroom, I am an avid project builder. I thrive in hands-on environments, from designing UAVs to writing simulation software. As the former Head of the Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (AUV) team, I led over 35 students in research and development, qualifying for international competitions like SAUVC Singapore and RoboSub USA.

When I'm not studying or working on projects, I enjoy stargazing, reading science fiction, and contributing to open-source projects.
